About Us

Welcome to Aromatica Urban!

Aromatica Urban was born out of our love for food and the vibrant urban atmosphere. Established in 2022, the restaurant has grown to become a favorite destination for those seeking an authentic and innovative dining experience. With its warm ambience and modern interiors, Aromatica Urban is the perfect place for business meetings, family events, or simply enjoying a relaxing dinner.

Layanan yang Ramah dan Profesional
